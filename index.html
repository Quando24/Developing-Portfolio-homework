About Me

My name is Raquan Stanley and I am from New York City, the Bronx specifically, born and raised. I currently live in Allentown, PA. After the first 14 years of my life, I moved to Cheltenham, PA, where I spent my 7th, 8th, and 9th grade years of school, at Cedarbrook middle school and Cheltenham high school, respectively. I moved back to the Bronx for the remainder years of my high school life, at a school named "Bronx high school for Writing and Communication arts". Following high school, I was accepted to multiple colleges, and I ended up attending Syracuse University, where I earned an Economics, BA degree.
